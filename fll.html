About

First Lego League, or FLL, is an international competition in which teams design, create, and program a robot using LEGO Spike to score points in the event challenge and create innovative solutions to a problem, all while being guided by the FLL Core Values. These three elements - the Robot Game, Project, and FLL Core Values - make up the yearly Challenge that focuses on a different real-world problem related to the sciences. Our FLL team will participate in one or more tournaments. FLL is also a very good background experience for those that want to participate in the FIRST Robotics Challenge (FRC) that is typically ran in high schools, or grades 9-12.

Anyone within the age requirements can join our team! There is no programming, designing, or other requirement. Students will be taught by award-winning Senseis that come from backgrounds of Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, and Design Arts. These Senseis are alumni of various local First Robotics Competition (FRC) teams including but not limited to Team 1403 Cougar Robotics, TEST Team 303, and Team 75 Robo Raiders.

FLL is a 3-month commitment. If you decide to leave the team mid-season, you are severly worsening the experience for the Ninjas and Senseis you are walking away from.

Our team is registered as FLL Team Somerset Samurais #55495 in Bridgewater, NJ.

For all Ninjas, FLL costs $249 per month (for 3 months) or $700 paid in full (discounted).
